The use of Artificial Intelligence such AI Chatbot in Education and learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has emerged as a significant development in the realm of education, particularly with the rise of Intelligent Tutoring Systems (ITS). These systems have gained prominence in various countries, serving as online resources that aim to enhance teaching and learning experiences. ITS offers personalized and convenient tutoring, overcoming geographical barriers and providing individualized support to students. The utilization of AI in these systems strives to create a stress-free and customized learning environment, catering to students' specific needs in a more accessible manner. The potential of this technology to transform education and foster inclusivity by providing equal opportunities to students worldwide is remarkable.

However, as with any technological advancement, there are important considerations and challenges to address. One crucial concern is ensuring academic integrity within the context of AI in education. The use of AI-based systems, including ITS, introduces potential risks such as cheating and plagiarism. Some AI systems have demonstrated impressive performance in exams, raising questions about maintaining fair and credible assessment practices. Striking a balance between leveraging AI for enriched educational experiences while upholding the integrity and authenticity of students' work is vital. Furthermore, the ongoing advancements in AI, including text-generating AI like ChatGPT, introduce new possibilities and implications in the field of education. Thorough examination of both the positive and negative aspects of these advancements is necessary to shape the future of education effectively.
